(13/04/2016) According to the feedback from students, we clarify the points about Question 10 that may be unclear as follows:

  1. You may find that there are some unusual Asia semesters in Semesters table. Thus, it is possible that two semesters have the same year and term. For example, for major semester 2007S1, we actually have two semesters, i.e., "Semester 1 2007" and "Asia Semester March 2007".A subject is considered to be offered in major semester 2007S1 as long as it was offered in either normal semester, e.g., "Semester 1 2007" or Asia semester, e.g., "Asia Semester March 2007". A subject is considered to be popular as long as it was taught in all major semesters from 2002 to 2013 (i.e., 02S1, 02S2, 03S1, 03S2, ..., 13S1, 13S2).You should identify the major semester based on its year and term, rather than its id or longname.
  1. A student is considered to have good performance on a subject as long as s/he once got a 'HD' or 'DN' on that subject.It does not matter whether s/he once got some other grades on that subject.
